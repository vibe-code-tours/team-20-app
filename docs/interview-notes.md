# User Interview Notes - Fundraising Website

## Who + when

- **User:** Fundraising Organizer
- **Date:** 15 June 2026
- **Interviewed by:** @sandarma

## What we asked + heard

- Current food orders are managed through Facebook Messenger and WhatsApp.
- Volunteers manually copy customer orders into spreadsheets.
- Payment screenshots are received through multiple chat conversations.
- Sometimes orders are missed or duplicated.
- Customers frequently ask whether their payment has been received.
- Preparing packing lists before the event takes several hours.
- The organizer wants one place to manage orders and payment confirmation.
- The most important feature is a simple ordering process that volunteers can learn quickly.

## Findings folded into DOD

Added the following Definition of Done items to `spec.md`:

- [ ] Customer can upload a payment screenshot.
- [ ] Organizer can manually verify payment.
- [ ] Organizer can update order status.
